<p><p>The fully compostable toothbrush market refers to toothbrushes that are made entirely from biodegradable materials, such as bamboo, cornstarch, or plant-based fibers. These toothbrushes can be disposed of in compost bins and break down naturally without leaving any harmful waste behind. On the other hand, handle compostable toothbrushes have handles made from compostable materials, while the bristles are typically made from a non-compostable material such as nylon. These toothbrushes still offer a more sustainable option compared to traditional plastic toothbrushes, but their handles can be composted while the bristles should be removed and disposed of separately.</p></p>
