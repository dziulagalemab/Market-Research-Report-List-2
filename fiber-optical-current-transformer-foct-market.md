<p><p>Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FOCT) is a device used for measuring electric current using fiber optic technology. It has two main market types: Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (EFOCT) and Flexible Electronic Fiber Optical Current Transformer (FEFOCT). EFOCTs are fixed devices that convert current into an optical signal for transmission. On the other hand, FEFOCTs are flexible and can be easily installed around irregularly shaped conductors. Both types offer accurate and reliable current measurement, catering to different installation requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>
