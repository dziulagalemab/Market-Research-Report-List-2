<p><p>A Maintenance Bypass Switch is an electrical device used to enable the maintenance or servicing of uninterruptible power supply systems (UPS) without interrupting power to critical loads. It allows for the safe transfer of power from the UPS to the utility power source, bypassing the UPS system during maintenance operations. This ensures uninterrupted power supply to critical equipment and prevents downtime and data loss.</p><p>The future of the Maintenance Bypass Switch market looks promising, with a projected CAGR of 11.4% during the forecasted period. The increasing adoption of UPS systems in various industries, such as IT & telecom, healthcare, and manufacturing, is the key driver for market growth. The need for continuous power supply, surge protection, and voltage regulation to safeguard critical operations is fueling the demand for UPS systems, consequently driving the market for Maintenance Bypass Switches.</p><p>Another factor contributing to market growth is the growing trend of digitalization and the increasing dependence on data centers.
