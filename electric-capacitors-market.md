<p><p>Electric capacitors are electronic components used to store and release electrical energy. The market consists of several types. Ceramic capacitors are commonly used for their small size and high capacitance. Film/paper capacitors offer high voltage ratings and low cost. Aluminium capacitors provide high capacitance and low leakage. Tantalum/niobium capacitors offer high capacitance and reliability. Double-layer/supercapacitors have high energy density and long cycle life. These capacitors serve various applications in different industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
