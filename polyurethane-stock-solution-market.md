<p><p>The polyurethane stock solution market is divided into two types: polyester type and polyether type. Polyester type polyurethane stock solution is made by reacting a polyester polyol with an isocyanate. It is known for its excellent mechanical properties, high resistance to chemicals, and good abrasion resistance. On the other hand, polyether type polyurethane stock solution is produced by reacting polyether polyol with an isocyanate. It is known for its exceptional flexibility, resilience, and low-temperature resistance. Both types find applications in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
