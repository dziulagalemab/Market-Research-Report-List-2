<p><p>The Silicon Carbide (SiC) Semiconductor market includes various types of SiC semiconductors. The 2H-SIC semiconductors are hexagonal in structure and possess unique electrical properties. 3C-SIC semiconductors are cubic in structure and are preferred for their compatibility with silicon substrates. 4H-SIC semiconductors have a wider bandgap and excellent thermal conductivity, making them suitable for high-power applications. 6H-SIC semiconductors exhibit high breakdown strength, making them ideal for high-voltage devices. Apart from these, there are other types that cater to specific industry needs, such as SiC metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors (MOSFETs) and Schottky diodes.</p></p>
