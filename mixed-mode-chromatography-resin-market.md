Hydrophobic mixed-mode resin exploits hydrophobic interactions to separate hydrophobic molecules. Hydroxyapatite mixed-mode resin utilizes the specific binding affinity of biomolecules with calcium phosphate. These different market types offer versatile options for researchers and biopharmaceutical manufacturers in achieving high-resolution purification and separation of target molecules.</p></p>
