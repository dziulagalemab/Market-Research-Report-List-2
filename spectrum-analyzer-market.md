<p><strong>The Spectrum Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Tuned</li><li>Vector Signal</li><li>Real-Time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectrum analyzers are used to measure and analyze the frequency spectrum of signals. Swept-Tuned spectrum analyzers work by sweeping through a specified frequency range, measuring and displaying the signal levels at each frequency point. Vector Signal analyzers, on the other hand, provide more advanced measurement capabilities by analyzing the complex modulated signals. They capture and measure both amplitude and phase information simultaneously. Real-Time spectrum analyzers offer the most advanced and comprehensive analysis capabilities as they can capture and process the signal in real-time, enabling a detailed analysis of time-varying signals and capturing intermittent events.</p></p>
