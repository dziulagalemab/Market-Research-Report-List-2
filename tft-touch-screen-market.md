<p><p>A TFT (Thin Film Transistor) touch screen is a type of display technology that combines a TFT LCD (Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display) panel with a touch-sensitive layer. TFT touch screens are widely used in various devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, automotive displays, and industrial control systems.</p><p>The TFT touch screen market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue its upward trajectory in the coming years.
