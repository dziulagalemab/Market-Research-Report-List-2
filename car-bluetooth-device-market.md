<p><strong>The Car Bluetooth Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC</li><li>DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car Bluetooth device market can be categorized into two types - AC and DC. AC refers to the alternating current market, where Bluetooth devices are powered by the car's built-in electrical system. These devices can be charged and operated using the AC power supply. On the other hand, DC refers to the direct current market, where Bluetooth devices are powered by a car's cigarette lighter or auxiliary power socket. These devices are specifically designed to work with the car's DC power source and do not require external charging.</p></p>
