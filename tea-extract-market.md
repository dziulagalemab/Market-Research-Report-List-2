<p><p>The tea extract market consists of three main types: functional component extracts, instant tea powder, and concentrated tea liquid. Functional component extracts are derived from tea leaves and contain specific bioactive compounds, offering various health benefits. Instant tea powder is made by extracting tea concentrate and then drying it into a powder form, allowing for quick and convenient preparation. Concentrated tea liquid is a highly concentrated form of tea that can be diluted with water or other beverages. All three types cater to consumers seeking different levels of convenience and functionality.</p></p>
