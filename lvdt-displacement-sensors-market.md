<p><strong>The LVDT Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) displacement sensors can be categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. </p><p>Contact sensors involve physical contact between the LVDT sensor and the object being measured. These sensors require direct contact to accurately measure displacement, making them useful in applications where the object's surface is accessible or when a high level of accuracy is required.</p><p>In contrast, non-contact sensors do not require physical contact with the object. These sensors use various techniques such as electromagnetic or optical principles to measure displacement without any touching. Non-contact sensors are advantageous in situations where the object is fragile, inaccessible, or in motion, as they offer non-invasive and highly accurate measurements.</p></p>
