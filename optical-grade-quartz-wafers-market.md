<p><strong>The Optical Grade Quartz Wafers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Side Polished</li><li>Double Sides Polished</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The optical grade quartz wafer market encompasses two main types: single side polished and double sides polished. Single side polished wafers undergo polishing on one side, leaving the other side untouched. This makes them suitable for applications where only one side needs to be transparent and smooth. On the other hand, double sides polished wafers are polished on both sides, resulting in improved optical quality and uniformity. These wafers find applications in industries where high precision and accuracy are required, such as optoelectronics and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
