<p><p>Electrical height adjustable tables in the market are available in two types: Single Motor and Dual Motor. Single Motor tables use one motor to adjust the height of the table, making them cost-effective and suitable for basic height adjustments. On the other hand, Dual Motor tables employ two motors to provide synchronized and stable height adjustments, ensuring a smoother transition and accommodating heavier loads. Dual Motor tables are preferred in high-intensity work environments where frequent adjustments and stability are essential.</p></p>
