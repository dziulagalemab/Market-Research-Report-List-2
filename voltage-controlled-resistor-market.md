<p><p>The voltage controlled resistor market includes two types: JFET and MOSFET. JFET (Junction Field-Effect Transistor) acts as a variable resistor due to its voltage-dependent conductivity, enabling the resistance to be controlled by an applied voltage signal. MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) operates similarly, with its resistance being controlled by voltage variations. Both types provide efficient and precise resistance control, making them suitable for various applications, such as electronic circuits, amplifiers, and digital systems. The voltage controlled resistor market offers these types based on customer preferences and specific application requirements.</p></p>
