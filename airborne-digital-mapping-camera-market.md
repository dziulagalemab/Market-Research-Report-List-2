<p><strong>The Airborne Digital Mapping Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom)</li><li>Area Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airborne Digital Mapping Camera market includes two types of technologies: Linear Array Scanners (Pushbroom) and Area Arrays. Linear Array Scanners capture images by scanning the Earth's surface with a single row of sensors, creating a continuous strip of data. This technology is capable of capturing high-resolution images with high ground coverage efficiency. On the other hand, Area Arrays capture images using a matrix of sensors, allowing simultaneous data capture from multiple rows. This technology offers the advantage of capturing images with high spatial resolution in a single frame.</p></p>
