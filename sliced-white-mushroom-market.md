<p><p>The sliced white mushroom market is divided into three main types: button, medium, and large. Button mushrooms are the smallest in size, typically about 1-2 inches in diameter, and are commonly used in a variety of dishes such as salads, soups, and stir-fries. Medium-sized mushrooms are slightly larger, around 2-3 inches in diameter, and are often preferred for grilling or stuffing. Large mushrooms, with a diameter of 3 inches or more, are ideal for filling with ingredients or serving as a meat substitute. Each type caters to different culinary needs and preferences.</p></p>
