<p><p>MCPCB stands for Metal Core Printed Circuit Board. There are three main types of MCPCB in the market - Aluminum Core PCB, Copper Core PCB, and Alloys Core PCB. An Aluminum Core PCB is made of an aluminum base layer, which offers good heat dissipation and is cost-effective. Copper Core PCBs have a copper base layer, providing excellent thermal conductivity and electrical performance. Alloys Core PCBs are made of a combination of metals, offering a balance between thermal conductivity, cost, and performance. These MCPCBs are widely used in applications where heat dissipation is crucial, such as LED lighting and power electronics.</p></p>
