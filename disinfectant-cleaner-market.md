<p><p>The disinfectant cleaner market encompasses various types of disinfectants that are used to kill or neutralize harmful microorganisms on surfaces. Chlorine disinfectants contain chlorine compounds that are effective against a wide range of bacteria, viruses, and fungi. Peroxide disinfectants use hydrogen peroxide to combat germs. Aldehyde disinfectants, such as formaldehyde, have strong antimicrobial properties. Alcohol disinfectants contain ethanol or isopropanol and are commonly used for hand sanitization. Iodine-containing disinfectants possess iodine as an active ingredient. Phenol disinfectants contain phenolic compounds and are effective against many pathogens. Other disinfectant types may include quaternary ammonium compounds or hydrogen peroxide-based products.</p></p>
