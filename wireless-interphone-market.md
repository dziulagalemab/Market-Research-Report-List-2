<p><p>The wireless interphone market consists of two main types: analog interphones and digital interphones. Analog interphones use traditional radio frequency technology to transmit and receive voice communication. They are relatively simple and cost-effective but have limitations in terms of audio quality and security. On the other hand, digital interphones utilize advanced digital signal processing techniques to offer improved audio clarity, enhanced features, and increased security. They are considered more modern and efficient, although they can be more expensive than analog interphones.</p></p>
