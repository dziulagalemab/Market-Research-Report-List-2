<p><p>Compression stockings, also known as compression socks or support stockings, are specialized tight-fitting garments designed to exert pressure on the legs and feet. These stockings are made up of elastic materials that apply graduated pressure, with maximum pressure at the ankles and decreasing pressure as they move up the leg. The purpose of compression stockings is to improve blood flow in the legs, prevent blood pooling, reduce leg swelling, and alleviate symptoms associated with various conditions such as venous insufficiency, deep vein thrombosis, varicose veins, and edema.</p><p>The future outlook of the compression stockings market appears promising.
