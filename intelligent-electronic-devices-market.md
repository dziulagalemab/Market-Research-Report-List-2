<p><p>Intelligent Electronic Devices (IEDs) are advanced devices used for monitoring, controlling, and protecting power systems. They are equipped with microprocessors and communication capabilities. The market for IEDs includes various types of devices. Digital relays offer protection by detecting faults in power systems and initiating corrective actions. PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers) are used for automating industrial processes. Load Tap Controllers regulate voltage in power transformers. Reclosers automatically restore power supply after a temporary fault. Smart meters measure and monitor electricity consumption. Other IEDs include devices like power quality monitors, synchrophasors, and fault recorders.</p></p>
