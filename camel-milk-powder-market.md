<p><ul><li>Full Cream Milk Powder</li><li>Skimmed Milk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Camel milk powder market consists of two main types: full cream milk powder and skimmed milk powder. Full cream milk powder is made by evaporating camel milk until it becomes a powder, while retaining its natural fat content. It is creamy and rich in taste, suitable for various uses like baking and cooking. On the other hand, skimmed milk powder is produced by removing the fat from camel milk, resulting in a low-fat product. Skimmed milk powder is often chosen by those who prefer a healthier option or are watching their fat intake.</p></p>
