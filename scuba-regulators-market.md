<p><p>Scuba regulators are essential equipment for divers, allowing them to breathe underwater. The first stage scuba regulator connects to the scuba tank, reducing the high-pressure air to intermediate pressure for delivery to the second stage. The second stage scuba regulator is the part divers breathe from, as it further reduces the pressure to ambient levels. Both the first and second stage regulators are crucial for ensuring consistent delivery of air while diving, offering safety and comfort to divers.</p></p>
