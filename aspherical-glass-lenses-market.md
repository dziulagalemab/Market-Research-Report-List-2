<p><p>The aspherical glass lenses market can be categorized into three types: can type, barrel type, and others. The can type lenses have a convex outer surface, resembling a can shape. They are commonly used in cameras and optical instruments. Barrel type lenses have a convex inner surface and are shaped like a barrel. They are widely utilized in photography and automotive applications. The "others" category includes various aspherical lens designs that do not fall into the can or barrel types, such as dual-sided aspherical lenses or freeform lenses.</p></p>
