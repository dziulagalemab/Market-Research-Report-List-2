<p><p>The dairy starter culture market involves different types of cultures used in the production of dairy products. Mesophilic cultures are commonly used in cheese production and ferment at lower temperatures. Thermophilic cultures work at higher temperatures and are used in the production of yogurt and other fermented dairy products. Probiotics are live microorganisms that confer health benefits and are added to dairy products for their positive effects on the digestive system. These different types of starter cultures cater to various dairy product processing needs and provide unique characteristics to the final products.</p></p>
