<p><strong>The Portable Load Bank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Load Bank</li><li>Reactive Load Bank</li><li>Resistive Reactive Load Bank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable load bank market comprises three main types: resistive load banks, reactive load banks, and resistive reactive load banks. </p><p>Resistive load banks are designed to test and provide a load to power sources such as generators or UPSs by converting electrical energy into heat. Reactive load banks, on the other hand, create a load that simulates the inductive or capacitive characteristics of electrical equipment. Resistive reactive load banks combine the features of both, offering a comprehensive testing solution for power equipment. These load banks play a crucial role in various industries to ensure the reliability and performance of power systems.</p></p>
