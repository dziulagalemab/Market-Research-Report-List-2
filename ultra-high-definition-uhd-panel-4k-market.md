<p><p>The Ultra High Definition (UHD) Panel (4K) market includes three main types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) panels use a backlight to create images, providing good color accuracy and brightness. LED (Light Emitting Diode) panels are a type of LCD that uses LEDs as the backlight, resulting in better energy efficiency and thinner displays. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) panels emit their own light, producing deeper black levels, wider viewing angles, and superior contrast compared to LCD and LED panels.</p></p>
