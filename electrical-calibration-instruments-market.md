<p><p>The electrical calibration instruments market can be divided into two main types: portable electrical calibration instruments and stationary electrical calibration instruments. Portable instruments are small, lightweight devices that can be easily carried to different locations for calibration purposes. They are often used by field technicians or engineers who need to perform on-site calibrations. On the other hand, stationary instruments are larger, more permanent fixtures that are typically installed in calibration laboratories or manufacturing facilities. These instruments are used for calibrating other electrical devices and ensuring their accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
