<p><p>The environmentally rubber process oil market consists of various types, namely TDAE, MES, NAP, RAE, and others. TDAE (Treated Distillate Aromatic Extract) is a non-carcinogenic and eco-friendly rubber process oil with low sulfur content. MES (Medium Extracted Solvates) is a sustainable process oil derived from renewable resources. NAP (Naphthenic) rubber process oil offers excellent low-temperature properties and is biodegradable. RAE (Residue Aromatic Extract) is a high-quality rubber process oil obtained from refining crude oil. Other types may include bio-based oils or specialty products tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
