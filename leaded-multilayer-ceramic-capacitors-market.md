<p><ul><li>Radial Lead Type</li><li>Axial Lead Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Leaded multilayer ceramic capacitors come in two main types: radial lead type and axial lead type. The radial lead type has leads that are attached to the sides of the capacitor, allowing it to be easily soldered on circuit boards. On the other hand, the axial lead type has leads that are attached to the ends of the capacitor, making it ideal for through-hole mounting. Both types serve the purpose of providing capacitance in electronic circuits, but their different lead placements cater to distinct mounting requirements.</p></p>
