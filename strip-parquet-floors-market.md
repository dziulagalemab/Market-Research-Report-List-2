<p><strong>The Strip Parquet Floors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engineered</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The strip parquet floors market includes two main types: engineered and solid. Engineered strip parquet floors are made by layering multiple thin wood veneers on top of each other, creating a stable and durable product. They are less prone to warping and can be installed in various locations, including basements. Solid strip parquet floors are made from a single piece of solid wood and offer a traditional and timeless look. They are typically thicker and can be sanded and refinished multiple times. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the flooring industry.</p></p>
