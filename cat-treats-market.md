<p><p>The cat treats market can be categorized into three types: dry cat treats, wet cat treats, and semi-moist cat treats. Dry cat treats, as the name suggests, have a crunchy texture and are generally made from grains, meat, and vegetables. Wet cat treats come in moist or soft forms and are usually packaged in cans or pouches. These treats are formulated with a higher moisture content and can be more palatable for cats. Semi-moist cat treats have a chewy texture and are typically made with meat by-products and additives to enhance their taste and texture.</p></p>
