<p><p>The Non-Fused Switch Disconnector market includes two main types: Rotary and Knife-blade. Rotary switch disconnectors have a rotating mechanism that allows for easy switching between different electrical circuits. They are commonly used for industrial applications where a high number of switching operations are required. On the other hand, knife-blade switch disconnectors have a blade-like mechanism that provides a secure disconnection of electrical circuits. They are commonly used in residential and commercial settings where safety and easy operation are important factors. Both types of switch disconnectors play a crucial role in controlling and disconnecting electrical power.</p></p>
