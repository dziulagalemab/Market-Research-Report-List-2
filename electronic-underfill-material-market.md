<p><strong>The Electronic Underfill Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Underfill Material (CUF)</li><li>No Flow Underfill Material (NUF)</li><li>Molded Underfill Material (MUF)</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Electronic Underfill Material market comprises three main types: Capillary Underfill Material (CUF), No Flow Underfill Material (NUF), and Molded Underfill Material (MUF). CUF is a liquid material that flows into the gaps between chips and substrates using capillary forces. NUF, on the other hand, does not flow and simply fills the gaps without the need for capillary forces. MUF is a solid material that is pre-molded and placed between the chips and substrates. These materials are used to enhance the reliability and performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
