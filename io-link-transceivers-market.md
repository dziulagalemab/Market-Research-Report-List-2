<p><p>IO-Link transceivers are used for communication between sensors, actuators, and controllers. Full Duplex IO-Link Transceivers enable simultaneous bidirectional transmission, allowing data to be sent and received at the same time. This enhances the speed and efficiency of communication. Half Duplex IO-Link Transceivers, on the other hand, allow data transmission in only one direction at a time. While they may not offer simultaneous transmission, they are still effective in facilitating smooth communication in cases where bidirectional communication is not required or feasible.</p></p>
