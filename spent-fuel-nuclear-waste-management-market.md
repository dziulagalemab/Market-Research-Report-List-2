<p><p>Spent Fuel & Nuclear Waste Management Market involves the management and disposal of different types of nuclear waste generated from nuclear power plants. The market is categorized into three types based on the level of radioactivity and the duration of containment required for safe storage. Low level waste contains minimal levels of radioactivity and can be safely managed through shallow land burial. Intermediate level waste contains higher levels of radioactivity and requires more extensive containment measures. High level waste, such as spent fuel, is highly radioactive and requires long-term storage in deep geological repositories to ensure its safety and protection of the environment.</p></p>
