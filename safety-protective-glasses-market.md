<p><p>Safety protective glasses can be categorized into two types: absorbent glasses and reflective glasses. Absorbent glasses are designed to absorb and dissipate harmful wavelengths, such as UV rays and infrared radiation, to protect the eyes from damage. On the other hand, reflective glasses work by reflecting dangerous light away from the eyes. Both types of glasses are essential for ensuring eye safety in various industries where workers are exposed to hazardous environments or activities. They provide an effective barrier against potential eye injuries and help maintain optimum eye health.</p></p>
