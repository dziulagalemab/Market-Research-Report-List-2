<p><p>Halal is an Arabic word that means "permissible" or "lawful" in Islamic dietary laws. Halal applies to various aspects of life, including food, beverages, personal care products, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. To be considered halal, products must not contain any ingredients from forbidden sources such as pork or alcohol and must be processed in accordance with Islamic rituals.</p><p>The global halal market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years.
