<p><p>The floating thermometer market consists of two types: digital floating thermometers and graduated floating thermometers. Digital floating thermometers use electronic sensors to measure temperature and display it on a digital screen. They are often waterproof and offer easy reading capabilities. Graduated floating thermometers, on the other hand, use a liquid-filled tube with marked graduations to indicate temperature. These thermometers are simple and affordable, often used for basic temperature measurement in swimming pools or aquariums. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs in the thermometer market.</p></p>
