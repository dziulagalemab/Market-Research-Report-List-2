<p><p>The Enhancement MOSFET transistor market consists of two main types: N-Type and P-Type. N-Type MOSFETs have a negatively charged channel region, allowing the current to flow when a positive voltage is applied. These transistors are commonly used for amplifying and switching applications. On the other hand, P-Type MOSFETs have a positively charged channel region, allowing the current to flow when a negative voltage is applied. They are often used in complementary circuits with N-Type MOSFETs, enabling efficient amplification and switching operations. Both types play a vital role in various electronic devices and circuits.</p></p>
