<p><p>RF adapters are used to enable connectivity between different types of RF coaxial connectors. In-series adapters connect two components with the same connector type, allowing for seamless integration. Between-series adapters connect two components with different connector types, bridging the gap between different RF cables or devices. T-shaped adapters have one common connection and two different connectors, allowing for versatile configurations. These adapters play a crucial role in ensuring compatibility and flexibility in RF systems.</p></p>
