<p><p>The solenoid valve connectors market can be categorized into two types based on voltage: AC voltage and DC voltage. AC voltage refers to the electrical current that frequently changes direction, commonly found in household power outlets. Solenoid valve connectors designed for AC voltage applications are compatible with this type of current. On the other hand, DC voltage refers to the electrical current that flows in only one direction, commonly used in batteries and electronic devices. DC voltage solenoid valve connectors are tailored to work with this constant flow of electricity.</p></p>
