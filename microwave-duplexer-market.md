<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Microwave Duplexer market can be classified into three types: Crystal Type, Ceramic Type, and Others. Crystal Type duplexers use a quartz crystal resonator to separate transmit and receive signals, providing high performance and stability. Ceramic Type duplexers use ceramic materials, offering better thermal stability and cost-effectiveness. The Others category includes alternative technologies such as surface acoustic wave (SAW) and micro-electro-mechanical systems (MEMS). SAWs offer compact size and low insertion loss, whereas MEMS enable miniaturization and integration. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in the Microwave Duplexer market.</p></p>
