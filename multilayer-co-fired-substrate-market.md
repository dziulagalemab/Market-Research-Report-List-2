<p><strong>The Multilayer Co-fired Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LTCC Substrate</li><li>HTCC Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multilayer co-fired substrate market involves two types of products: LTCC substrate and HTCC substrate. LTCC stands for Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic, which is a type of multilayer substrate made from ceramic material. It offers advantages like excellent electrical performance, high frequency, and low signal loss. HTCC, on the other hand, stands for High-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic. It is also a multilayer substrate made from ceramic, but can withstand higher temperatures. Both types are widely used in various industries including telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive, for their superior electrical properties and performance.</p></p>
