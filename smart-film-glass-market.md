<p><strong>The Smart Film Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Film Glass market consists of three main types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic Smart Glass is a type of glass that can change its transparency when a voltage is applied, providing privacy and energy efficiency. SPD Smart Glass, also known as Suspended Particle Device, can alter its transparency by controlling the alignment of particles within the glass. PDLC Smart Glass, or Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal, uses a special film to switch from opaque to transparent when an electric current is applied.</p></p>
